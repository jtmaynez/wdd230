Site Name

Liberty Lake Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the city in which I live and where the chamber should operate.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to have the City of Liberty Lake have it's own Chamber of Commerce site. Currently they are sharing with the neighboring city. With their own site they can better connect with other business within their own city. This site will provide access to valuable resources, discounts, and relationships.

Scenarios

  • How many business are in this Chamber of Commerce?
  • Who is on the chamber's board of directors and what company are they affiliated with?
  • When and where are the events taking place?
